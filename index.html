About

Girls Run the World is an independent sub-unit of the Girls Who Code Summer Immersion Program at Microsoft Bay Area Summer 2017.

Made by 4 amateur coders, this website's goal is to help people of all ages understand the severity of the discrimination women face around the world and the discrimination that may be occuring next to you.
